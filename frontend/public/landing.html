Not a single number. Every analysis returns a label, a calibrated probability, a confidence band, per-layer signals, and a plain-English explanation.

0→1

Calibrated Probability

A true probability from the isotonic-calibrated meta-classifier. 0.85 means 85% of texts with this score are actually AI. Not a score — a probability.

High (≥ 0.60), medium (≥ 0.30), or low. Tells you at a glance how certain the system is, without reading the raw number.

Measured Performance

Every number is
from real evaluation.

No marketing estimates. Every metric below comes from a held-out test split the model has never seen, with git SHA and dataset hash for reproducibility.

99.71%

Reliability

Accuracy on definitive (AI/HUMAN) predictions. 2 errors out of 1,720 on the held-out test split.

86.0%

Coverage

Fraction of inputs receiving AI or HUMAN label. The other 14% are short texts (< 50 words) routed to UNCERTAIN.

0.9965

F1 Score

On a 2,000-row held-out test split that the model never saw during training, validation, or threshold fitting.

0.9986

Recall

Fraction of AI text correctly caught. Only 7 misses out of 996 actual AI samples on the test split.

0.9526

F1 (Adversarial)

On 3,482 rows including adversarial subsets designed to fool the detector. The hard benchmark.

0.0048

Brier Score

Calibration quality — lower is better. The isotonic calibrator achieves 14x improvement over the fixed-weight baseline.

<200ms

p95 Latency

CPU-only inference. Warm cache. No GPU required for production text detection.

10rps

Stable Throughput

Per pod, concurrency=4. Measured under sustained 15-minute load with zero errors.
